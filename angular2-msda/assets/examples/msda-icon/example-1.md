### Properties

#### `name(string)` option
Name under which the icon should be registered.

---
#### `width(number)` option
Width of the icon.


---
#### `height(number)` option
Height of the icon.


---
#### `color(string)` option
Color of the icon.


---
#### `shadow(number)` option
Shadow of the icon.


---
### Usage



```html
<msda-icon name="culture" width="48" height="48" color="#FFFFFF" shadow="1">
</msda-icon>
```
