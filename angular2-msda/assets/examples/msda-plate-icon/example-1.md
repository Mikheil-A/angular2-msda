### Properties

#### `name` option
Name of icon.

---
#### `type` option
Type of icon (circle or square)


---
#### `width` option
Width of icon.


---
#### `height` option
Height of icon.


---
#### `color` option
Color of icon.


---
#### `background` option
Background of icon.

---
#### `shadow` option
Shadow of icon.


---
### Usage

```html
<msda-plate-icon name="icon-4" type="circle" color="#FFFFFF" background="lightBlue" shadow="1">
</msda-plate-icon>
```
